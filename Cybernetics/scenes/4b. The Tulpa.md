### 4b. The Tulpa

But thought-forms can get more complex. Stack enough of them and you get a tulpa.

A tulpa is a *character*. It has collectively agreed-upon traits, behaviors, an archetype — defined well enough that anyone who's "downloaded" it can run a copy in their own imagination. And the more you replay that character and the more mass media reinforces it, the deeper it installs into your subconscious.
You download hundreds of these a day without noticing. Your favorite TV character is a tulpa. That streamer you've never met but feel like you know — you're running a model of them in your head that predicts what they'd say. That's a tulpa too. None of them asked permission.
