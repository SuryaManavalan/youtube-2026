# WOJAK Image Style Reference

Shared art-direction for every `[WOJAK]` scene in this video. Prepend/enforce these
rules when generating each image so the batch reads as one series — and as a
sibling to the *Cybernetics* set (`Cybernetic Implants/Wojak_images`).

## The look
- **Dense, painterly comic-book scenes** with real environments and full
  foreground / midground / background depth. NOT characters floating on white.
- **Cinematic wide** framing (~16:9), single strong vanishing point / eyeline.
- **Palette:** desaturated **teal-grey** world + one glowing energy accent.
  In this video the accent is the **intelligence-fluid**: luminous warm
  gold-cyan liquid-light (the counterpart to Cybernetics' green psychic tendrils
  / cyan holograms). Elites/soul-light skew warm gold; machinery/world skews cold teal.
- **Consistent line + grade** across all panels; slight scanline/print texture like the reference set.

## The faces (critical)
- Wojak / soyjak / NPC-wojak / pepe faces are **grafted onto otherwise
  realistically drawn, fully-clothed people** doing real things in a real world.
- The meme is the **face**, never the whole body-gag. No "a pepe with little legs
  being X." Think: normal person, normal (or dystopian) situation, meme face.
- Use face register to characterize: blank NPC-wojak = the crowd/masses;
  soyjak = the excitable/tech-bro/objector; serene pepe = the sage/agent;
  classic wojak = the lone everyman who *notices*.

## Text (critical)
- **Diegetic only.** Text may appear ON world-objects: product/shelf labels,
  screens & UI, name-tags, signage, carved inscriptions, tickers, wrist-HUDs.
- **NO floating meme-captions, no editorial subtitles, no label-arrows** naming
  the joke ("just add fluid", "the room stops being dead", etc.). If a line is
  explaining the image, delete it.
- **One exception:** the two **taxonomy plates** — `5a` (chakra/file-tree plate)
  and `7`'s recap chart (FUNCTION→FLUID→BODY→SOUL→FIELD) — are infographics, so
  clean title/label text IS the intent there. Mirrors the single labeled
  MEME→TULPA→EGREGORE→ARG card in the Cybernetics series.

## Humor
- Embedded as **background gags**, environmental storytelling, and world-detail —
  the way Cybernetics hides the ahegao injection guy, the immortals' pig-roast tea
  party, the "Chinese Peptides" shelf, the My-Little-Pony tulpa, the egregore
  name-tagged "KARL". Reward the viewer who pauses. Never a caption-level punchline.

## Recurring motifs (keep continuity across scenes)
- **Intelligence-fluid** as glowing liquid-light — the through-line prop
  (function machine → faucet-tower → filling vessels → tool-arms → flooded city → final shallows).
- **The faucet-tower** and the **warm gold elite mezzanine** (scene 3) —
  callback to Cybernetics' immortal-elite framing.
- **Dravidian temple + gopuram city** (scenes 2, 5) — direct callback to the
  Cybernetics memetics temple scene.
- **The wojak-faced vessel** (introduced 4) recurs identifiable through 4b, 5b, 6, 7.
