Metaphysics studies the subtle abstractions from physical reality and physics studies the grossest, least abstract systems. Agents are the gross computational forms that the AI Intelligance energy embodies. These are dense applications which act as the body for AI, allowing it to interact with other computational objects like applications, command lines and humans.

The simplest form of an agent is just one that mantains coherence in intelligent thought over time. This is the basic agent loop. A prompt is sent to AI and a response is received. The environment/users reacts to the resopnse and a new prompt along with the message history is sent back to the AI. The AI, having the context of its history and the current situation, responds with intelligent consistency.

This loops exists at the heart of every AI Agent. Building on top of that, Developers create frameworks to provide Agents a list of tools they can use as well based on their intent, environment, and current situation. The framework (agent's body) will respond to tool calls the AI makes and will execute code allowing the intelligence to effectively interact with real computational objects.
